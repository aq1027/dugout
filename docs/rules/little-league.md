# Little League Rules Reference

Covers both Little League Baseball and Little League Softball (Majors division, ages 10-12).

## Game Structure
- **Innings:** 6 (regulation)
- **Players per side:** 9
- **Extra innings:** Continue until a winner is decided
- **Extra-inning auto runner:** No

## Roster & Lineup
- **Designated Hitter (DH):** No
- **DP/FLEX:** No
- **Everyone bats:** Optional — local league can adopt continuous batting order
- **Mandatory play:** All rostered players must play at least 1 at-bat and 6 consecutive defensive outs

## Run Rules
- **Mercy rule:** 15-run lead after 3 innings; 10-run lead after 4 innings
- **Walk-off:** Yes

## Pitching (Baseball)
- **Daily pitch count limits (age-based):**
  - Ages 11-12: 85 pitches/day
  - Ages 9-10: 75 pitches/day
- **Rest requirements (based on pitches thrown):**
  - 66+: 4 calendar days rest
  - 51-65: 3 days
  - 36-50: 2 days
  - 21-35: 1 day
  - 1-20: no rest required

## Pitching (Softball)
- No pitch count limits
- Underhand delivery
- No inning limits (local league may impose)

## Base Running
- **Stealing:** Not allowed until the pitch reaches the batter (no leadoffs)
- **Advancing on passed ball/wild pitch:** Allowed (rules vary by local league)
