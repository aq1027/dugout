# NCAA Baseball Rules Reference

## Game Structure
- **Innings:** 9 (regulation)
- **Players per side:** 9
- **Extra innings:** Continue until a winner is decided
- **Extra-inning auto runner:** Yes — runner placed on 2B starting in the 10th inning (conference rules may vary)

## Roster & Lineup
- **Designated Hitter (DH):** Yes
- **DP/FLEX:** No
- **Everyone bats:** No

## Run Rules
- **Mercy rule:** 10-run lead after 7 complete innings (or 6½ if home team leads)
- **Walk-off:** Yes

## Mound Visits
- Governed by conference rules; generally more relaxed than MLB

## Pitch Clock
- **Bases empty:** 20 seconds (varies by conference)
- **Runners on:** Not universally enforced at NCAA level
